Daily Tips

The Plate Method

Fill half your plate with non-starchy vegetables, one quarter with lean protein, and one quarter with carbohydrate foods.

Counting Carbs

Carbohydrates impact your blood sugar the most. Always check the nutrition label for the "Total Carbohydrate" amount per serving size.
